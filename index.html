ABOUT ME

My name is Lauren Eaton and I am originally from Ilkeston, Derbyshire, but now live in Nottingham. I graduated from the University of Leeds in 2018. I have made a career mostly in translation and localisation, utilising my BA Japanese Studies degree to assist in Japanese to English projects of all sizes and categories.
Since I was a teenager I have been involved with the charity 'STAMMA' through various fundraisers and events, showing support for other young-adults who have struggled with stammering from childhood, as I have, and helping them gain the confidence and desire to speak oepnly outside of speech therapy.
